About This Project

This web app explores financial and economic trends for selected REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts) using real-time data from the twelvedata.com API.

Other economic factors come from the St. Louis Federal Reserve Bank's FRED API.


Developed for my INST377 final project, this site helps users explore connections between real estate investments and economic indicators such as CPI, interest rates, and unemployment.
